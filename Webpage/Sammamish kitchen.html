Our Story

INDIAN BY NATURE

Name says it all: Our head culinary experts are Super Moms, who have combined Cooking Experience of over 65 years. Our team had locally owned and operated different successful restaurants for last 20 years. Now was the time for us to serve our community with North-Indian Home-style cuisine. So Mommy's Kitchen came to life, and we are excited to be offering great food at a fair price for Take-out, Delivery and Catering.
Concept of Mommy’s Kitchen is simple: making Food available at your Finger Tips. We work hard in the Kitchen, so you don’t have to. Sit back, relax and order food that is not only healthy and delicious but convenient as well.
